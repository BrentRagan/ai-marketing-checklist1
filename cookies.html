What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly:

  • Session management - Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Form submissions - Process your lead magnet requests
  • Security - Protect against spam and malicious activity

Analytics Cookies

We use these to understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics - Track page views, bounce rates, and user behavior
  • HubSpot Analytics - Monitor lead magnet performance and conversion rates
  • Website performance - Identify areas for improvement

Marketing Cookies

These help us deliver relevant content and track marketing effectiveness:

  • HubSpot Tracking - Personalize content based on your interests
  • Lead magnet optimization - A/B test different versions for better results
  • Retargeting - Show relevant ads on social media platforms
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag - Track professional audience engagement

Preference Cookies

These remember your choices to improve your experience:

  • Language preferences - Remember your preferred language
  • Form pre-filling - Save time by remembering your information
  • Theme selection - Remember if you prefer our professional or modern design

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Opt-Out Options

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • HubSpot: Manage preferences in any email we send you
  • LinkedIn: Ad preferences in your LinkedIn account settings
